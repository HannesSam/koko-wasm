Dietrich Frenzen was a wizard who was hired as a Quidditch referee in or prior to 1994.

Physical appearance
Frenzen had short white hair and silvery-white stubble covering his rather tan, round face. His prominent cheekbones and dark, bushy eyebrows gave an impression of pride, and his dark eyes shone.
Etymology
Dietrich is the German form of Theodoric, which in turn means "ruler of the people"[2][3].
