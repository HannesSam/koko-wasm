Quong Po (1443 – 1539) was a Chinese wizard and dragonologist, who studied the Chinese Fireball breed and discovered the magical uses of their powdered eggs.[2][1]
Behind the scenes
In the Norwegian version of the video game adaptation of Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets, he is identified as Chong Po.
