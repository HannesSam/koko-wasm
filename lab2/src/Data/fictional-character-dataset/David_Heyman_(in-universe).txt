David Heyman was an American No-Maj and the commissioner of the New York Police Department in 1926.[1][2]

Biography
He signed a wanted poster issued for Newt Scamander and Jacob Kowalski on 6 December 1926. It advised that, in the event of the pair's capture, he should be immediately contacted by telephone at 3100 Heyman.[1][2]
Behind the scenes
This character was named after David Heyman, the producer of all eight Harry Potter films, as well as Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
