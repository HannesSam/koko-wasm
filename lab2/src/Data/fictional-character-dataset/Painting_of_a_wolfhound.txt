A painting of a wolfhound was hung at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, near Sir Cadogan's portrait.[1]
History
In 1995, when Harry Potter was on his way to Divination class, Sir Cadogan tried to follow him by running through a neighbouring picture. However, the large and angry-looking dog inhabiting the painting chased him back into his own portrait.[1]
