Biography
He interviewed a witness who saw a building get destroyed by an Obscurus in New York in 1926.[1]
After interviewing, he asked a photographer colleague if he felt like getting a drink, to which the photographer replied that he had promised Martha (presumably his wife or girlfriend) that he would abstain from consuming alcohol.[1]
Behind the scenes
The reporter was portrayed by Tristan Tait in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
