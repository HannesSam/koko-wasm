The Fates (also known as Moirai) was a group of three weaving Greek goddesses who assign individual destinies to mortals at birth. Their names were Clotho (the Spinner), Lachesis (the Alloter) and Atropos (the Inflexible).[1] Professor Trelawney claimed have gotten information about the future Divination examination from them in June 1994.[2]
