Biography
When Harry Potter visited Arthur Weasley with the Weasleys at Christmas 1995, the Welcome Witch appeared to be a typical secretary type; somewhat rude, brief, and monotonous, which was likely used to be humorous when Harry Potter read a sign, reading "Our Welcome Witch will be happy to assist you".[1]
Physical description
She was a squat little woman with short, blonde hair and blue eyes.[1]
