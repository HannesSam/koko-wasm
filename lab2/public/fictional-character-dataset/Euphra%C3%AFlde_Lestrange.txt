Euphraïlde Lestrange (1692 – 1755) was a French witch and a member of the pure-blood Lestrange family. Euphraïlde died in 1755 and was laid to rest in the Lestrange family Mausoleum in Cimetière du Père-Lachaise, Paris, France.[1]
