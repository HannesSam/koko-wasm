"Un magicien Animagus dans l'affaire du canard"
— Le Cri de la Gargouille, 1927[src]
This individual was a wizard and an Animagus who lived in the early 20th century. In 1927, Le Cri de la Gargouille reported that he was somehow implicated in the "affaire du canard".[1]
Behind the scenes
The newspaper title that mentions this individual is probably a pun: "canard", which means "duck" in French, can also refer to an untrustworthy newspaper, or a false or misleading report.
