Johan Hoffman was a wizard and professional Seer, who made a prophecy in January of 1665. His prophecy was stored in the Hall of Prophecy in the Department of Mysteries of the British Ministry of Magic.[1]
Behind the scenes
He may have been the Unidentified male Seer whose prophecy was knocked over by Bellatrix Lestrange and Lucius Malfoy in the Hall of Prophecy.
