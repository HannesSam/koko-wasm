M. Carneiro was a wizarding linguist, and author of Grammatica, a grammar compendium.
Behind the scenes
This character was presumably named after Mauricio Carneiro, who worked as a costume illustrator for every Harry Potter film from Goblet of Fire onward, and as a costumer for Order of the Phoenix and Half-Blood Prince.[1]
