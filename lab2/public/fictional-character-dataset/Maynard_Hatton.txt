Maynard Hatton was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. During the 1996–1997 school year, he ran a Duelling Club in the Training Grounds, which included Fergus Cowley and Anthony Otterburn as members.[3]
