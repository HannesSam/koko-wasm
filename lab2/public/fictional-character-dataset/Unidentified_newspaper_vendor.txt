This male newspaper vendor was selling copies of the Daily Prophet in the Atrium of the Ministry of Magic on the day of Harry Potter's disciplinary hearing.[1]
Behind the scenes
The vendor was portrayed by Jamie Wolpert in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix.
