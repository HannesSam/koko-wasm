This individual was a witch at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, who was a member of Slytherin house.[3]
Biography
During the 1996–1997 school year, Malfoy had Goyle turn into this girl via Polyjuice Potion to be able to guard the Room of Requirement while Malfoy was repairing the Vanishing Cabinet inside.[2]
