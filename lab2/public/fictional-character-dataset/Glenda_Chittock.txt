Glenda Chittock (b. 1964) was a British witch who was host of the Wizarding Wireless Network programme, Witching Hour. Being host of this enormously famous radio programme, she is featured on a Chocolate Frog Card.[1]

Behind the scenes
If Glenda attended Hogwarts, her first year would have been the Marauders' fifth year.
Etymology
"Glenda" is a name created in the 20th century from the Welsh elements glân "pure, clean" and da "good".
