This individual was a Russian wizard who attended Koldovstoretz, in Russia.[1]
A skilled potioneer, the individual was elected to represent the Koldovstoretz at the Wizarding Schools Potions Championship, in which he competed against students from Mahoutokoro School of Magic, Uagadou School of Magic and Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.[1]

Biography
Koldovstoretz
The Russian wizard attended Koldovstoretz for his magical education.[1]
Wizarding Schools Potions Championship
The wizard enters the battlefield
The wizard became a skilled potioneer, and at age 17 or above competed in the Wizarding Schools Potions Championship for the golden cauldron, where he represented Koldovstoretz, his school. In the championship, the wizard arrived at the battlefield first, and attempted to cross. However, he disturbed the Red Caps, who knocked him out unconscious. Afterwards, the Hogwarts potions champion used a Beautification Potion to scare the Red Caps away.[1]
Physical appearance
The student had light skin, with dark hair and chiselled features. He was thin, and wore purple robes with white fur lining and a white fur hat.[1]
Magical abilities and skills
Potions: As a skilled potioneer, he was highly talented at making potions.[1]
Behind the scenes
This is the first and, thus far, only student of Koldovstoretz introduced throughout the Harry Potter series.
