This centaur was a member of the Forbidden Forest centaur colony.[1]
Biography
During the Skirmish in the Forbidden Forest in 1996, this centaur stood at the back of the herd of centaurs that tried to execute Harry Potter and Hermione Granger, when he suggested that the two teenagers "pay the consequences". Fortunately, all the centaurs were chased away by Grawp.[1]
