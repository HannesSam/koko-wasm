These individuals were students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. They fought during the Battle of Hogwarts in 1998 against the Death Eaters, but then died on the Seventh Floor.[2]
Behind the scenes
They were most likely killed during the first part of the battle, as they were lying on the corridor.
Possibly one of them killed the Unidentified male Death Eater killed on the Hogwarts Seventh Floor.
