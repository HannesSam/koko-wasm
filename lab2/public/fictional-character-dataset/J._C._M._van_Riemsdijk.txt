Jonkheer Johan Cornelis Marius van Riemsdijk (16 December 1841 – 30 June 1895) was a Dutch Muggle musician and author.[1][2][3]
A copy of his book Het Stads-Muziek-Collegie te Utrecht (Collegium-Musicum-Ultrajectinum): Eene Bydrage tot de Geschiedenis der Toonkunst in Nederland could be found in the Hogwarts library by the 1990s.[3]
