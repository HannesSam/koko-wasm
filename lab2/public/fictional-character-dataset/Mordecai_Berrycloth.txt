Mordecai Berrycloth was a British wizard employed by the Ministry of Magic in London in the capacity of Auror,[1] under head of department, Harry Potter.
Biography
In the 2010s, Mordecai Berrycloth visited the Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures, where he held a course in combative magic for their field members; Magizoologist members of the Statute of Secrecy Task Force were also in attendance.[1]
