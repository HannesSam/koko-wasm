Biography
He and fellow officer caught Newton Scamander and Jacob Kowalski believing that they were robbing a jewellery store before they saw a lion that escaped from the Central Park Zoo, leaving the two suspects to escape.[1]
Behind the scenes
The Policeman was portrayed by Bart Edwards in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
