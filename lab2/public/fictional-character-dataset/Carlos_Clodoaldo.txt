Carlos Clodoaldo[1] was a Brazilian wizard and a Beater for the 2014 Brazilian National Quidditch team.
Biography
According to Ludovic Bagman in a report to the Daily Prophet, American Beaters Picquery and Pringle would have to "find better form" if they were to best the Brazilians Santos and Clodoaldo in their semi-final match on 4 July, 2014.[2]
