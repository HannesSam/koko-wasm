These nineteen individuals (fl. 1997) were wizards who worked at the Ministry of Magic under its Death Eater rule.
Biography
They adulated Minister for Magic Pius Thicknesse excessively. On the morning of 2 September, 1997, these individuals followed Minister Thicknesse around Level One of the Ministry.[2]
