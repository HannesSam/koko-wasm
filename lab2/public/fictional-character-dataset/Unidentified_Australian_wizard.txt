This individual was an Australian Wizard who was among the patrons at the Blind Pig while Newton Scamander, Porpentina Goldstein, Queenie Goldstein, and Jacob Kowalski were negotiating with Gnarlak for information on the location of some of his escaped beasts in 1926.[1]
Behind the scenes
The Australian Wizard was portrayed by Nicholas McGaughey in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them.
