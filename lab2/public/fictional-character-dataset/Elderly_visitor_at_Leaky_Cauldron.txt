This man was an elderly wizard who visited the Leaky Cauldron in 1991 and welcomed Harry Potter back to the wizarding world.[1]
Behind the scenes
In the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, he is the first wizard in the Leaky Cauldron to welcome Harry, instead of Dedalus Diggle in the novel.[2]
