This individual was a wizard who was assigned to guard the entrance of Gringotts Wizarding Bank in 1998 during the Death Eaters' reign over the wizarding world. He and Marius used Probity Probes to search anyone attempting to enter. Both guards were Confuded by Harry Potter on 1 May, 1998 when he and his friends broke into the bank to steal one of Voldemort's Horcruxes from the Lestrange Vault.[1]
