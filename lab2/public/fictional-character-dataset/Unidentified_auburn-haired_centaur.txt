This centaur lived in the Forbidden Forest in the 2000s.[1]
Biography
On 2 September, 2008, he was attacked by two wizards or witches when they were found by Ivy Warrington and another student, who helped him get rid of the attackers. Later, he returned the favours by aiding a battle between them and a Norwegian Ridgeback. After they won, the centaur warned the students of the perils in the Forbidden Forest and advised them to be brave and honest.[1]
