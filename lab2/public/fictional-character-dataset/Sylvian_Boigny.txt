Sylvian Boigny was an Ivorian witch who played as the Seeker for the 2014 Ivorian National Quidditch team. During the first match of the 2014 Quidditch World Cup against Norway, Boigny was beaten by her opposite Sigrid Kristoffersen to the Golden Snitch after 128 minutes of play, securing a win for Norway.[2]
