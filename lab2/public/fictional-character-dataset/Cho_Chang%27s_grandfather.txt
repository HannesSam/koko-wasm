Biography
In 1927, he was one of the Aurors led by Theseus Scamander who were summoned to break-up Gellert Grindelwald's rally at the Lestrange Mausoleum in Paris, France.[1]
Chang was Cho Chang's paternal grandfather.[2]
Behind the scenes
He is played by Simon Wan in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.[4]
