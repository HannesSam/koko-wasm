Atsushi Takagi was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.[3]

Etymology
Atsushi is a male name of Japanese origin. Takagi is a common Japanese surname, meaning "tall tree".
Behind the scenes
Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Japanese descent.
