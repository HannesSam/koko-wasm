This wizard smuggled Muggle artefacts, enchanted them and sold them back to Muggles during the 1990–1991 school year.[1]
Biography
During the 1990–1991 school year, this wizard sold a cursed kettle to an oblivious Muggle. The wizard was seen by a witness on the Charing Cross Road and also outside the Leaky Cauldron.[1]
Jacob's sibling helped investigate and track down the wizard as a part of the Ministry of Magic student programme, and at the end of the assignment, Alastor Moody reported that he would soon be apprehended.[1]
