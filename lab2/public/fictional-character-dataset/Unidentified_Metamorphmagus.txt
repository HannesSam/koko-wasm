This wizard or witch was a Metamorphmagus and infiltrated the British Ministry of Magic Headquarters during the 1990–1991 school year, disguising as several individuals including Reginald Cattermole, Amos Diggory and Nymphadora Tonks.[1]
After Jacob's sibling updated Corey Hayden with the events, Corey suspected the Dark Witch from Knockturn Alley to be this Metamorphmagus due to the known transfiguration of her in the previous school year.[2]
Magical abilities and skills
Metamorphmagus: This individual possessed the natural born ability to magically change all features of their physical appearance at will. They used this ability to impersonate various individuals so they could infiltrate the British Ministry of Magic.[1]
