L. Wakefield was a Muggle who worked as a professional illustrator. Wakefield created the illustrations that were used on promotional posters for an adapted production of Romeo and Juliet that was staged at the Regency Playhouse in the summer of 1997.[1]
Behind the scenes
This character was presumably named after Lauren Wakefield, a graphic artist who worked on Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince, and both parts of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows.
