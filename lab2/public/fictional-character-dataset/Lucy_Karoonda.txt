Lucy "Lucky" Karoonda was an Australian witch who played as Seeker on the Australian National Quidditch team. She was also this team's Captain.[1]
During the 1996–1997 school year, Karoonda's Chocolate Frog Card could be won by beating Patrick Fincher at a Quidditch challenge.[2]
