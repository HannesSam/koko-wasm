Sophie Berger was the sports reporter for an unidentified French-language wizarding newspaper in the 1920s.[1]
She wrote an article about Beauxbatons Academy of Magic being at the "head" of the European Quidditch League that was published in a 1926 issue of the newspaper.[1]
